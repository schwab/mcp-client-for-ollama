# Test Dialogue for ACCENT_WRITER

## Character Profiles (to be learned by agent)

**Lady Ashford** (Formal, educated aristocrat):
- Should use: proper grammar, no contractions, complex vocabulary
- Should say: "I am afraid", "rather", "quite", "indeed"
- Should NOT say: "yeah", "gonna", "ain't"

**Jake** (Casual, street-smart):
- Should use: slang, frequent contractions, dropped g's
- Should say: "yeah dude", "ain't", "gonna", "like"
- Should NOT use: formal language like "indeed" or "rather"

## Test Dialogue - Scene 1 (CONSISTENT)

Lady Ashford looked down her nose at the young man. "I am afraid I cannot permit such behavior in my establishment."

Jake shrugged. "Yeah, whatever. I'm outta here anyway."

"Indeed," Lady Ashford replied coolly. "That would be most prudent."

## Test Dialogue - Scene 2 (INCONSISTENT - Characters Swapped)

Jake straightened his collar and spoke carefully. "I believe we should proceed with the utmost caution in this matter."

Lady Ashford laughed. "Dude, you're totally overthinkin' this. Let's just do it!"

## Test Dialogue - Scene 3 (INCONSISTENT - Lady Ashford talks casual)

"So anyway," Lady Ashford said, "I'm gonna head over to the market and grab some stuff. Wanna come?"

Jake nodded thoughtfully. "I would be most honored to accompany you on your errand."

## Test Dialogue - Scene 4 (MIXED - Some consistent, some not)

Lady Ashford considered the proposal. "That is rather an interesting suggestion."

Lady Ashford frowned. "But like, isn't it kinda risky though?"
